Toast Sandwich recipe

Description

This is how to make a toast sandwich, great for finishing off a loaf of bread. recipe from Here

Prep Time: < 1 min

Cook Time: 2-4 min

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Optional: Butter or margerine

Steps

  1. Place 1 piece of toast in the toaster.
  2. While toast is in the toaster, butter 2 additional pieces of bread. Make sure to only butter one side of each piece so to not get butter on your hands when consuming.
  3. When the toast is done cooking, place the warm bread between the 2 buttered pieces of bread so that the butter on each uncooked bread slice is in contact with the face of the piece of toast.
